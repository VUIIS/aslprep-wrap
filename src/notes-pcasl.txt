        perf/

            sub-<label>_ses-<label>_acq-<label>_asl.nii.gz
            sub-<label>_ses-<label>_acq-<label>_m0scan.json
            sub-<label>_ses-<label>_acq-<label>_m0scan.nii.gz

subject, session from XNAT. acq label from scan ID of the ASL? E.g. 401? acq is optional here.
